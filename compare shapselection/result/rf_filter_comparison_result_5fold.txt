To evaluate the effect of feature filtering on model performance, we compared Random Forest models before and after applying the filtering process using 5-fold cross-validation and paired t-tests.
The differences in average performance for Accuracy, MCC, and ROC-AUC were -0.32%, -0.59%, and +0.02%, respectively. However, none of these differences reached statistical significance (p > 0.05).
These results suggest that the proposed feature filtering method can reduce feature dimensionality without compromising model performance.

Feature Filtering 기법이 모델 성능에 미치는 영향을 분석하기 위해, 랜덤 포레스트(Random Forest) 모델을 대상으로 Feature Filtering 적용 전후의 성능을 5-fold 교차검증과 쌍체 t-검정을 통해 비교하였다.
